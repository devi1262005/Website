To Love and To Be Loved

There's this fruit vendor on my daily route. Amazing how a simple smile can lead to free fruits and good vibes. Missing those moments a lot.

The Finish Line

Wrote this one about life's endings—it's kinda scary, yet there's this strange excitement.

Slaughter

While I was writing this, some kids were waving and smiling at me. I cannot even find this writing sad anymore because of that memory, I smile whenever I read this.

First year

Reminiscing about the times I struggled in class. Love sharing stories about the past

Don't Reread

Named it after a day I dissociated in class. Someone noticed I looked like I lost everything. Tried to crack a joke, but words failed me at that moment.

Mountains and I

My first-ever piece. No fancy stuff, just my raw thoughts. Can't bring myself to change it—it's like my baby.

Festives

Its about festivals and people,the adults were always the same, the kid never noticed it, but now that she is an adult she does notice.

Will it rain today?

I love everything about rain, except for the streets, that get nasty with plastic bags.

Why Fake Emotions?

Most people are so strong, so good at pretending. I just wrote it when someone told me to always smile, but I wanted to be sad, so I can let go.

Unintentional Forgetfulness and Regret.

I struggle to respond to texts at home due to personal reasons. It leaves me feeling overwhelmed.

Sky.

I love lying down, absorbing fleeting moments, watching clouds drift, stars twinkle, and patterns emerge. Some nights, I dream of stars, longing to see them before sleep.

18 Year Old Bed

Turning thoughts into four stanzas. Fond of my furniture—they've seen me through thick and thin, my closest confidants.

Forgiver

Forgiving takes a lot of energy, and I do it so I can move on. It takes a lot of maturity to be honest.

Routines

I have a love-hate relationship with routines. Mostly hate. Repetition feels suffocating, limiting my freedom.

Stay

Scream

Sway

The yearning for love, recognition, and understanding is both a tragedy and a bliss. The simple desire for connection can profoundly shake a person, altering their core values.

They who

Observations, from within and from everywhere.Of the people who no longer recognise themselves.

Hamster

My hate for routines, but I am talking to the hamster on wheels.

Life

Bluff

Lovers
