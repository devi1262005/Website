Existence,
To exist, is so painful.
It's so dreadful, that I cannot
Breathe without thinking about the
People I suffocated with my presence,
I hate myself so much,
That I cannot exist within myself.


I wish to jump out of skin,
Crawl out of my body,
Every ounce of this pain is so true.
I cannot feel anything towards me,
I cannot understand my pain,
I cannot hear myself,
I cannot see myself,
I cannot stop the tingling numbness of my skin,
I cannot stop faking that I can.


I am split,
I exist in the mirror,
I exist in the air,
I exist in everyone's eye,
Yet I do not in mine.
I do not exist in my life,
I cease existing,
When I'm not around anyone.
I am a concept,
A concept that replays
Over and over,
However, the way you perceive.


I cannot stand the thought of
Existing,
Being perceived,
I wish to be in camouflage,
Wish to vanish into thin air,
Wish to never show myself to the world,
Wish to never speak,
Wish to never hear myself,
Wish to never ever exist,
I exist to ruin,
I exist to unexist.


To be or not to be?
I do not know,
I am a being,
For another being,
And I do not be,
For me.
