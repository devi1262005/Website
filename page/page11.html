To all the people that I forgot unintentionally,
Those who could have wondered,
"What happened to this girl who wouldn't stop talking?"
I'm sorry for falling out suddenly,
I could have been tired,
Or supporting the weight of my limbs mentally,
While my body couldn't.

I could be running on low energy,
While my mind is racing with thoughts,
On how should I be and what I should be during that time.
I did not mean to forget you,
Your presence in my life is not insignificant,
I value you so much,
Even if my actions don't mean the same.

Some days, I forget myself,
How to eat, how to sleep,
How not to just be a starfish,
As I lie awake in my bed,
Not being able to move my limb,
I think of you, and when I do it's already a month late,
What should I say then? I just apologetically,
Seemingly unapologetic, delete the message,
Pretending it doesn't exist,
Like my will to go on some days.

The excuses I spew, laughable to my own ears,
I wonder how convincing they seem to you?
I know how my mind would be racing with thoughts,
Had I been ignored like that,
I hope you do not value a person like me so much,
Spare yourself of the pain.

And as I go on wondering about my ability to make
Meaningful connections,
I look at the people who could have been my friends,
If I had mustered up the energy to just talk, each day,
Reached out when they did,
Sounds like I took people for granted,
Turned friends to acquaintances,
Ruining things, what I do the best.

Some of the forgotten acquaintances, forever remembered,
Always forgotten during the times I should have remembered.
Bitter-sweet memories, hardly sweet,
Some of the others' existence though often remembered,
When I run into them and not otherwise,
Shows how much of a terrible being I am.

I apologize terribly,
You do not deserve someone like me,
She who lets herself get consumed,
Not replying to a concerned friend,
Doesn't deserve a friend at all,
But you still reply back whenever I message you anyway,
Thank you for always reaching out,
I apologize if I ever made you doubt your place in my life,
I always hold you close to my heart,
Even if I do not seem so.

Even these excuses sound pathetic to my own ears,
For, if I wanted to I could have,
But that's really not the case,
I really want to, but mustering up energy,
Requires energy, this doesn't mean you are consuming,
It's me, the weight of me consuming myself,
That everything else darkens out,
Like my memory and the ability to just open up.
I'm sorry, you deserve better,
And I know there are better people than I for you,
And I'm thankful for that.
