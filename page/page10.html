Is fake happiness better than real sadness?
Can't be faithful to yourself,
Why do you neglect yourself,
Just because other people do?

Why would you put yourself through that?
Have you ever thought about
How it is so much easier to cry,
Than just smile when you couldn't?
You are still sad,
Why not let go?

Why not muster up the courage?
It must be difficult in front of other people,
Who do not put the effort to know you,
Stay when happy, leave when sad,
Those are the people around you,
Then why can't you be the person you need, for yourself?

Break when alone,
It is okay to break when alone,
Put on a facade for others,
Do not put a facade for yourself,
See you, in the mirror,
Do not see what everyone else sees.

Break down willingly,
Instead of breaking unwillingly,
So that you would have courage to build yourself up,
Instead of turning deaf to your own weeps.
Tread on a weak foundation,
That could be improved,
Do not tread on a shaky foundation,
That could break anytime,
Though looking perfect.
