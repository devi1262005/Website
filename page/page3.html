I try to survive;

Like the goat that doesn't know it's about to be killed,

Like the mice that doesn't know it's about to get trapped,

Like the clown that doesn't know that they are being laughed at,

So full of clues,

So full of problems,

So full of hopes,

So full yet never enough,

No matter what role I play,

Nothing ever fits,

Fate a cruelly crafted circle.
