When we first turned our pages together,
we laughed, made promises.

With each new turn,
was a spur of happiness,
we had never imagined it,
had never braced ourselves,
there was sunshine,
then came the moonshine,
your love had withered,
While I, lingered in the moonlight.


Perhaps, that is why you decided
to turn our final pages.
Have you no reason to warn me?

You were there this time again,
as a grim stranger,
with broken promises,
and unsaid words,
the final page fluttered away,
when you swiftly walked out,
of us.
