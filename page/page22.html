Earth, creates,
Creatures, survive,
Humans, exploit.

Wonder if this the way it should have been,
That the mountains, trees, oceans,
Were they formed to quench thirst?
Give shade? Provide a place to rest?
Or were they just formed simply because it could be?


Pondering this way,
Makes me realise that maybe we were too created
To just simply exist, be what we are, truly,
Without looking for purpose aimlessly,
And maybe we would realise our true purpose,
Or our purpose could just find us,
Much like how we found oceans, trees,
For us, and call it the nature's purpose,
Is it truly?


Would we call nature's elements beautiful,
If they have no purpose to us?
Doesn't this show the marred view of humanity?
Goodness of a person, always determined by
How helpful they are,
Simply existing humans, are unfit, lazy.


By just staying, loving and giving,
We could find our purpose,
Just maybe,
Or call this a bluff,
I do not know.
