Dreading the days,
Treading through time,
As each day quickly passes by,
Never understanding how I held through.


When a time once lived,
Reaches its end,
Marked as done,
I find myself living those days
In my head,
In solitude,
The days dreaded becomes the desired.


I find myself living those days
Looking back with a longing gaze,
Gushing about it in a daze,
Immortalizing the times lived,
A foolish endeavor that
Gives me peace.


Perhaps, it's the comfort of known ending,
Rather than pondering the impending doom,
Mind never set in the present,
Always floating in the past,
An utter imbecile,
That wouldn't survive a day
If she actually lived, the day.
