Turbulent mind,
Turbulent mind,
midst the stilled life,
unchanged, as then,
as now.


The mind that races,
creates a turbulent world,
and races against it,
but life around is always still.


The worn, out restless body,
that does not indulge itself,
in the placid world,
clutches itself at night,
tossing and turning,
as it braces itself,
for another restless day.


The life as stilled as ever,
the wind as breezy as ever,
the sun as warm as ever,
the birds as chirpy as ever,
the turbulent mind, as worried as ever.


All that cannot be helped,
'It is a fast-paced world',
it says to itself,
as it frightens itself,
unmoving as it lies,
awake in the nightmare,
it created for itself.


The body wishes for,
flower-bed and stars,
the mind races about,
death-bed and scars,
as they race against,
the tranquil world.
