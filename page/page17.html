Scream of the heart,

Screams in the void,

None present,

No listening ears,

No understanding eyes,

But the heart screams anyway.

The heart that hopes, screams,

The lips that contort,

Hoping to convey half the weight of the heart,

To better everything,

Instead every scream sucked by the walls,

Pair of eyes, ears,

Moving mouths, none understanding,

Make everything worse for the screaming heart,

It starts to bleed instead.
