Will it rain today?
Would it present itself with new matters to worry?
Would I find myself once again lost?
Would it present me with new routines?
Would I have to sail in deeper depths?
Are the sharks waiting?
Is it never-ending?
Would I count every second of this?
Hoping for the light in the depths?


Will it rain today?
Would it bring me immense joy;
And make me look up;
Enjoy as it pours and it pours?
Would I join my hands together hoping to get
Enough of it all?
Would it be blissful?
Would I count every second of this?
Hoping for forever?
Would it make me greedy till I drown?


Would I rather be the drenched?
Or the deflector?
Dawn to dusk I ponder;
"Will it rain today?".
