Sway,

so easily swayed,

like the entire colony surrounding

a granule of sugar,

like the pet that runs after a

ridiculous object, upon hearing "fetch."

The sway that gets the best of us,

that you never look past,

like the rat that walks into its trap,

the insatiable desire

to be felt, seen, and understood.

Never makes the best of us realize,

till the end of it is seen through,

with a deeper scar, that requires more tending,

You let yourself fly again,

like the dried leaf on the ground,

everywhere but nowhere.
