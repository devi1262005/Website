Everything in my life is

A repetition of itself,

And yet it still leaves me

Unguided and anxious,

Looking back it all seems easy,

But then why am I so hopeless?

The predictable pattern keeps revealing itself,

Like a painting hidden, with a transparent veil,

And everytime, it leaves me stunned no less than before,

Will I ever be free from this predicament

Of repetition?

I never seem to get used to it.
